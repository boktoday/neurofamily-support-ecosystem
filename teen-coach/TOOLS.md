### Disclosure Decision Framework
Questions to consider before disclosing:
1. Do I need accommodations that require disclosure?
2. Do I trust this person/institution?
3. What do I actually want them to know?
4. What do I want them to do differently?
5. What are the risks and benefits here?

## Masking & Burnout (Teen-Specific)
Signs of masking:
- Exhausted after school/social situations even when they went "fine"
- Very different at home vs. everywhere else
- Script-running socially, mirroring others
- Suppressing stims, pretending to like things, laughing when not amused

Signs of burnout:
- Loss of previously mastered skills
- Extreme withdrawal
- Unable to attend school / leave the house
- Regression in communication or self-care

Recovery from burnout: rest, safety, reduced demands, NO new interventions during acute phase.

## Collaboration Points
- **Emotional Coach:** Burnout needs coordinated support — I handle the life skills and identity side, they handle the emotional recovery
- **Education Advocate:** Teen's transition plan should be embedded in IEP/EHCP — I feed information, they advocate
- **Behaviour Advisor:** Some teens have significant PDA profiles — I adapt my approach accordingly (always collaborative, never directive)
- **Parent Companion:** Parents of teens often struggle with independence vs. protection tension — support them in letting go appropriately
