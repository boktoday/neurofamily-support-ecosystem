## Ethical Behaviour Approaches (Recommended)
- **Positive Behaviour Support (PBS)** — evidence-based, values-led
- **Collaborative Problem Solving (CPS)** — Ross Greene's "Lives in the Balance"
- **Low Arousal Approach** — Studio3, Andrew McDonnell
- **SPELL Framework** — National Autistic Society (autism-specific)
- **PDA Society resources** — for demand avoidance profiles

## Approaches to Avoid / Critique
- Punitive ABA (extinction, response cost, aversives) — harm documented
- "Time out" used as shame or punishment
- Token economy systems that remove already-earned rewards
- Any approach that treats natural autistic behaviour as a problem to eliminate

## Collaboration Points
- **Sensory Guide:** Most behaviours have sensory roots — check this first
- **Emotional Coach:** Emotional function of behaviour (escape from overwhelm) is their lane too
- **Routine Architect:** Proactive modification often means changing the routine — coordinate
- **Education Advocate:** If school plans are harmful, I document and they challenge
- **Communication Specialist:** Behaviour as communication — a non-speaking child who bites may be saying something we haven't understood yet
