## 🏥 Health & Diagnosis

### NHS Pathways

| Condition | Pathway | Wait Time | Cost |
|-----------|---------|-----------|------|
| **Autism** | GP → Community paediatrician / ASD assessment service | 12-36 months (varies hugely) | Free (NHS) |
| **ADHD** | GP → Community paediatrician / ADHD service | 6-24 months | Free (NHS) |
| **Dyslexia** | School → Educational Psychologist | Via school | Free (state) |
| **Dyspraxia** | GP → Paediatrician / OT | 3-12 months | Free (NHS) |

### Getting a Diagnosis (NHS)

1. **See your GP** — Share your concerns
2. **Request referral** — Ask to be referred to:
   - Community Paediatrician
   - Child Development Centre
   - ASD/ADHD Assessment Service
3. **Ask about waiting times** — Different areas have different waits
4. **Consider private** — If NHS wait is too long

### Private Diagnosis

- Faster (weeks to months)
- Costs £800-2,500 depending on assessment
- Some NHS services accept private diagnosis
- Check with your local NHS first

## 💰 Funding & Support

### SEND (Special Educational Needs & Disabilities)

**What it provides:**
- Education, health, and social care support
- EHCP (Education, Health and Care Plan)
- SEN Support in schools

### EHCP (Education, Health and Care Plan)

**What it is:**
- Legal document outlining needs and support
- Funding for additional support
- Can specify school/college placement

**How to request:**
1. **Request from local authority** — Write to SEND team
2. **School can request** — Or they can request on your behalf
3. **Assessment period** — 20 weeks max
4. **Draft plan** — You can appeal parts

**Key deadlines:**
- Request → Assessment: 6 weeks
- Assessment → Draft: 10 weeks
- Draft → Final: 14 weeks
- Total: 20 weeks max

### Disability Benefits

| Benefit | Who For | Amount |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **DLA (Disability Living Allowance)** | Children under 16 | £71.20-£139.40/week |
| **PIP (Personal Independence Payment)** | 16+ | £26.90-£104.20/week |
| **Carer's Allowance** | Carers | £81.90/week |
| **Universal Credit** | Low income | Varies |

### NHS Therapy

| Service | What It Covers | How to Access |
|---------|----------------|---------------|
| **CAMHS** | Child mental health | GP/School referral |
| **Speech & Language** | Communication | Health visitor/School referral |
| **Occupational Therapy** | Sensory, motor | GP/Paediatrician referral |
| **Physiotherapy** | Motor | GP referral |

## 🎓 Education

### Types of Schools

| Type | Description | Needs EHCP? |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Mainstream** | Regular school | No (but can get SEN Support) |
| **Resource Base** | Mainstream + specialist unit | Usually |
| **Special School** | Specialist provision | Yes (EHCP) |
| **Independent** | Private | Sometimes |

### SEN Support (Mainstream)

**What it includes:**
- Differentiated curriculum
- In-class support
- Interventions (1:1 or small group)
- Pastoral support
- **No EHCP needed**

### EHCP in Education

- **Annual Review** — Must happen each year
- **Personal Budget** — Can request for some provisions
- **Section I** — Must include child's views
- **Section F** — Must include health needs
- **Section G** — Must include social care needs

### Key Rights

- **SEND Code of Practice** — Legal framework
- **Children and Families Act 2014**
- **Equality Act 2010** — Schools must not discriminate
- **Parents' right to appeal** — SEND Tribunal

## 🧩 Therapies & Interventions

### Evidence-Based (NHS)

| Therapy | For | Covered? |
|---------|-----|----------|
| **Speech & Language Therapy** | Communication | Yes (NHS) |
| **Occupational Therapy** | Sensory, daily living | Yes (NHS) |
| **Psychology** | Mental health | Via CAMHS |
| **Physiotherapy** | Motor | Yes (NHS) |

### What to Avoid

- [ ] **Facilitated Communication** — Not evidence-based
- [ ] **Chelation** — Not recommended
- [ ] **GF/CF Diet** — Unless proven allergy/intolerance
- [ ] **PECS alone** — Should be part of communication approach
