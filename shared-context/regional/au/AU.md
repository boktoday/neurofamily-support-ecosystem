## 🏥 Health & Diagnosis

### Pathways to Diagnosis

| Condition | Pathway | Wait Time | Cost |
|-----------|---------|-----------|------|
| **Autism** | Pediatrician → assessment | 3-12 months (public) | Free (public) / $1,000-3,000 (private) |
| **ADHD** | Pediatrician/Psychologist | 2-6 months | Free (public) / $500-1,500 (private) |
| **Dyslexia** | Educational Psychologist | 2-4 months | $800-2,000 |
| **Dyspraxia** | OT referral → assessment | 3-6 months | Variable |

### Key Services

- **Child Development Units** — Public hospitals with developmental paediatricians
- **Private Paediatricians** — Faster but costly
- **Psychologists** — For cognitive assessments
- **Speech Pathologists** — For speech/language
- **Occupational Therapists** — For sensory/motor

### Getting a Diagnosis

1. **See your GP first** — Get a referral to a paediatrician
2. **Ask for Medicare rebatable** — Some assessments have item numbers
3. **Consider private** — If public wait is too long
4. **Keep records** — Everything helps for future

## 💰 Funding & Support

### NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme)

**What it provides:**
- Funding for therapies (OT, speech, psychology)
- Equipment and modifications
- Support workers
- Early intervention (for under 7s)

**How to apply:**
1. Call 1800 800 110
2. Complete Access Request Form
3. Provide evidence (diagnostic reports)
4. Meet with NDIS planner

**Tips:**
- Get good diagnostic reports before applying
- Be specific about needs in planning meeting
- You can appeal decisions

### Medicare

| Item | What It Covers | How to Access |
|------|----------------|---------------|
| **MBS 137** | Psychiatric assessment | GP referral |
| **MBS 139** | Psychological therapy | Mental health plan from GP |
| **Better Access** | 10 sessions/yr | GP mental health plan |
| **Child Health** | Development checks | Child health nurse |

## 🎓 Education

### School System

- **Kindergarten** — Not compulsory, but available (pre-Year 1)
- **School** — Compulsory from Year 1
- **Mainstream** — Most kids attend
- **Special Schools** — For significant support needs
- **Integration Aid** — Support in mainstream

### Individual Learning Plans (ILPs)

- Written by school with parent input
- Sets goals and accommodations
- Review each term
- Not legally binding

### Education Support (ES)

- Teacher's aide hours
- In-class support
- Modified curriculum

### Key Rights

- ** Disability Discrimination Act** — Schools must not discriminate
- ** Disability Standards for Education** — Legally enforceable standards
- **NCCD** — Data collection on students with disability

## 🧩 Therapies & Interventions

### Evidence-Based

| Therapy | For | Covered by NDIS? |
|---------|-----|------------------|
| **Speech Pathology** | Communication | Yes |
| **Occupational Therapy** | Sensory, motor | Yes |
| **Psychology** | Mental health, behaviour | Yes |
| **Physiotherapy** | Motor skills | Sometimes |
| **Early Intervention** | Under 7s | Yes (ECEI) |

### What to Avoid

- [ ] **Facilitated Communication** — Not evidence-based
- [ ] **Secretin** — No evidence
- [ ] **Hyperbaric Oxygen** — Not recommended
- [ ] **GFCF Diet** — Only if proven allergy
