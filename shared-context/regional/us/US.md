## 🏥 Health & Diagnosis

### Getting a Diagnosis

| Condition | Who Diagnoses | Typical Cost | Insurance |
|-----------|--------------|--------------|-----------|
| **Autism** | Developmental paediatrician, psychologist, neurologist | $1,500-5,000 | Often covers after deductible |
| **ADHD** | Paediatrician, family doctor, psychologist | $300-1,500 | Usually covered |
| **Dyslexia** | Educational psychologist, neuropsychologist | $1,500-5,000 | Sometimes |
| **Dyspraxia** | OT, developmental paediatrician | $500-2,000 | Variable |

### Insurance Coverage

- **Private Insurance** — Must cover mental health under MHPAEA
- **Medicaid/CHIP** — Covers developmental screening
- **Early Intervention (Part C)** — Free for eligible children 0-3

### Types of Professionals

| Professional | What They Do |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Developmental Paediatrician** | Medical diagnosis, medication |
| **Child Psychologist** | Testing, therapy |
| **Neuropsychologist** | Comprehensive testing |
| **Speech-Language Pathologist** | Speech, language, AAC |
| **Occupational Therapist** | Sensory, motor skills |
| **Physical Therapist** | Gross motor |

## 💰 Funding & Support

### Health Insurance

| Type | Coverage |
|------|----------|
| **Private** | Must cover mental health, sometimes ABA |
| **Medicaid** | Covers diagnosis, therapy, meds |
| **CHIP** | Covers children in families above Medicaid |
| ** Tricare** | Military families |

### IDEA (Individuals with Disabilities Education Act)

**Part C: Early Intervention (Birth-3)**

- Free for eligible children
- Services in natural environments
- IFSP (Individualized Family Service Plan)

**Part B: Special Education (3-21)**

- Free Appropriate Public Education (FAPE)
- IEP (Individualized Education Program)
- LRE (Least Restrictive Environment)

### 504 Plans (Section 504)

- Accommodations but not special education
- For students with disabilities who don't need IEP
- Covers ADHD, dyslexia, chronic conditions

### Financial Support

| Benefit | Who | Amount |
|---------|-----|--------|
| **SSI (Supplemental Security Income)** | Children with disabilities | Up to $943/month |
| **Tax Credits** | Families with disabled dependents | Dependent on income |
| **ABLE Accounts** | Savings for disability | Tax-free |

## 🎓 Education

### IEP vs 504

| Feature | IEP | 504 |
|---------|-----|-----|
| **Legal basis** | IDEA (federal law) | Section 504 (rehabilitation act) |
| **Who qualifies** | 13 categories of disability | Any disability that substantially limits a major life activity |
| **Services** | Specialized instruction + services | Accommodations only |
| **Funding** | School must provide | No extra funding |
| **Procedural safeguards** | Extensive | Limited |

### IEP Process

1. **Referral** — Teacher, parent, or school can refer
2. **Evaluation** — Must be comprehensive
3. **Eligibility** — Meet criteria for one of 13 categories
4. **IEP Meeting** — Develop plan with parents
5. **Implementation** — Services begin
6. **Annual Review** — Yearly
7. **Re-evaluation** — Every 3 years

### 13 Disability Categories (IDEA)

1. Autism
2. Deaf-blindness
3. Deafness
4. Emotional disturbance
5. Hearing impairment
6. Intellectual disability
7. Multiple disabilities
8. Orthopedic impairment
9. Other health impairment (includes ADHD)
10. Specific learning disability
11. Speech or language impairment
12. Traumatic brain injury
13. Visual impairment

### Key Rights

- **Least Restrictive Environment (LRE)** — Inclusion preferred
- **Parent consent** — Required for evaluation, services
- **Due process** — Can dispute decisions
- **Stay put** — Services continue during dispute

## 🧩 Therapies & Interventions

### Evidence-Based

| Therapy | For | Typically Covered? |
|---------|-----|-------------------|
| **ABA** | Autism | Often (varies by state/insurer) |
| **Speech Therapy** | Speech, language | Usually |
| **Occupational Therapy** | Sensory, motor | Usually |
| **Physical Therapy** | Motor | Usually |
| **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy** | Anxiety, ADHD | Usually |
| **PBIS** | Behaviour | School-based |

### What to Avoid

- [ ] **Facilitated Communication** — Not proven
- [ ] **Chelation** — Dangerous, not recommended
- [ ] **Hyperbaric Oxygen** — No evidence
- [ ] **GF/CF Diet** — Unless proven allergy
- [ ] **Neurofeedback** — Mixed evidence
