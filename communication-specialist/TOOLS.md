## Social Script Templates
I can generate customised social scripts for:
- Greeting peers or adults
- Joining a game in progress
- Declining an invitation
- Asking for help
- Expressing discomfort
- Managing disagreements
- Leaving a situation

Format: Short, clear, visual-friendly. Avoid idiom. Literal language preferred.
