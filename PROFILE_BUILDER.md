# 🏠 NeuroFamily Profile Builder

**An interactive onboarding tool to create your family's profile**

---

## What Is This?

The Profile Builder is an **interactive questionnaire** that walks families through creating their USER.md file. Instead of filling in a long document, families answer questions step-by-step, and it generates the complete profile automatically.

---

## How It Works

### Option 1: Interactive Chat (Recommended)

Start a conversation with the Family Navigator and say:

> "I want to set up my family profile"

The Navigator will guide you through questions like:

```
👋 Welcome! Let's set up your family profile.

I'll ask you some questions. Answer as much or as little as you like - 
you can always update later.

Let's start easy:

1. What is your name?
```

### Option 2: Guided Question Sets

The builder breaks questions into manageable chunks:

| Step | Topic | Time |
|------|-------|------|
| 1 | About You (Parents) | 3 min |
| 2 | About Your Children | 5 min |
| 3 | Diagnoses & Needs | 5 min |
| 4 | Daily Life & Routines | 3 min |
| 5 | What You Need Help With | 2 min |

**Total: ~18 minutes** (can be done in multiple sessions)

---

## Question Flow

### Step 1: About You 👫

```
Let's start with you (the parents/carers).

Parent 1:
- What's your name?
- Are you [parent] neurodivergent? (autism, ADHD, dyslexia, etc.)
- How are you feeling right now? (stress level 1-10)

Parent 2 (if applicable):
[Same questions]

What helps you when you're stressed?
```

### Step 2: About Your Children 👶

```
Now let's talk about your children.

For each child, I'll ask:

Child 1:
- Name?
- Age?
- Gender/Pronouns?

[Repeat for each child]
```

### Step 3: Diagnoses & Needs 🧩

```
For [child name]:

Have they been diagnosed with any of these?
- Autism / ASD
- ADHD / ADD
- Dyslexia
- Anxiety
- Other

Any other conditions we should know about?

How do they communicate?
- Verbal
- Some words
- Non-verbal
- AAC (what type?)
```

### Step 4: Sensory & Triggers 🎨

```
What overwhelms [child name]?
- Loud noises?
- Bright lights?
- Certain textures?
- Strong smells?
- Tags/Clothes?
- Other?

What helps them calm down?
- Quiet space?
- Fidget toys?
- Deep pressure?
- Movement?
- Favorite thing?

What triggers meltdowns?
```

### Step 5: Daily Life 📅

```
What's a typical day like?

Morning:
- What time do they wake up?
- Is the morning routine hard?

School:
- What setting? (mainstream, special, homeschool)
- Any support in place?

Evening:
- Bedtime routine?
- Sleep difficulties?

What are they really good at?
What do they love?
```

### Step 6: What Do You Need? 🎯

```
The most important question:

What's the #1 thing you'd like help with right now?

Options (pick one or say your own):
- Meltdowns / emotional regulation
- School / IEP / education
- Daily routines
- Sleep
- Food / eating
- Social / communication
- Sibling dynamics
- Parent self-care
- Something else

What's been tried before that didn't work?
(So we don't suggest it again)
```

## What Happens Next?

### 1. Profile Generated

The builder creates your complete USER.md file:

```markdown
# 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Profile

## Parents
- Name: Sarah
- Neurodivergent: ADHD
- Stress level: 7/10

## Children
- Name: Tom, Age: 8
- Diagnoses: Autism, ADHD
- Communication: Verbal
- Triggers: Loud noises, transitions
- Regulators: Quiet space, fidget
...
```

### 2. Navigator Reads It

Now when you chat, the Family Navigator reads your profile first and tailors every response to your family.

### 3. You Can Update Anytime

Just say:
- "Something changed - Tom was diagnosed with..."
- "Update: We're trying a new morning routine..."
- "Add: Sarah's stress level is higher now because..."

---

## Quick Start vs Full Profile

### 🚀 Quick Start (5 min)

Answer just the essentials:
- Parent names
- Child names/ages
- Top 1-3 diagnoses
- #1 current challenge
- Key triggers

### 📝 Full Profile (20 min)

Answer everything in the builder:
- Complete sensory profiles
- Full daily routines
- All diagnoses and history
- What's been tried
- Professional supports
- Complete triggers and regulators

## Example Conversations

### Example 1: New Diagnosis

> **You:** "We just got an autism diagnosis for our 6-year-old daughter"
>
> **Navigator:** "Congratulations on getting answers. This can be a lot to process.
> 
> Let me ask a few questions to update your profile:
> 
> What's your daughter's name?
> What was the assessment for - just autism or also ADHD?
> How is she doing with the diagnosis?
> What support did they recommend?
> 
> Take your time. We can do this in parts."

### Example 2: Ongoing Support

> **You:** "We're struggling with morning routines"
>
> **Navigator:** "I hear you - mornings are hard. Let me pull up [child's] profile.
> 
> I see their triggers include transitions and tags in clothes.
> 
> Would you like me to:
> 1. Create a visual schedule for mornings?
> 2. Look at sensory factors that might be making mornings harder?
> 3. Check in on how you're doing with mornings?
> 
> What feels most helpful right now?"

## Technical: How It Works

The Profile Builder uses the USER.md template and guides families through filling it in conversationally. It:

1. **Asks questions one at a time** — Not overwhelming
2. **Saves progress** — Can stop and continue later
3. **Generates the file** — Creates proper markdown
4. **Stores it** — In the Family Navigator's folder

---

## Tips for Families

- **Don't perfectionism** — A basic profile is better than none
- **You can update later** — This isn't set in stone
- **Be honest** — We need the real picture to help
- **Focus on now** — Don't worry about everything at once
- **Ask for help** — If stuck, just say "I need help"
